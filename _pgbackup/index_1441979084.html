Features

Say hello to our Digital Public Services Platform.



Service Definition

Define the Service’s Business Process

A robust drag ‘n drop business process editor that uses open standards, to draw and define the service’s process that is then run by the platform.

Define the steps that citizens and staff go through to fulfill the service. Use the Process Definition to define the process, from the initial citizen submission to the back-office tasking required to fulfill the service request from the citizen or business.

Experience Designer

Government standards compliant web forms

Design and build the experience in each step of the Service’s process. Use the drag ‘n drop web form builder, based on web industry web standards, to associate forms with each task and step in the service definition ensuring a robust service experience.

Support any device, meet accessibility requirements (WCAG 2.0 AA+), support multiple languages, and maintain security.

Citizen and Business Engagement with MyAccount

Seamless engagement from start to finish through a single platform

Engage with citizens and businesses:

  • Support any device
  • Provide seamless multi-channel Interactions
  • Start a service through any channel
  • White-label service discovery portal
  • White-label knowledge base
  • Service bundling
  • Assisted self-service
  • Support for drafts and save progress to complete later

Reporting

Reports, Dashboards, and Insights into services, government, and process.

Services are complex processes that require analysis, follow-up, and reporting

  • Robust web reporting on all aspects of the platform
  • Activity and security logs
  • Citizen profiles, interactions and channel usage
  • Service usage, costs and performance
  • Staff performance
  • Service and organizational KPIs and SLAs
  • Service bundling and demographic metrics
  • Business transformation opportunities
  • Performance alerts and notifications
  • Seasonal, time, and location analysis
